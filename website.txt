Project Name: ZEAL by Kasana Builder - Clone Project
Objective: Generate an exact replica of the premium commercial real estate landing page for "ZEAL by Kasana Builder", mimicking a high-end Lovable.app output.

1. GLOBAL THEME & VIBE
- Industry: Premium Commercial Real Estate / Grade A Workspaces.
- Vibe: Ultra-modern, luxurious, professional, and sleek.
- UI Style: Clean, minimalist with generous whitespace, high-contrast sections, and glassmorphism elements.

2. COLOR PALETTE
- Primary Background: Obsidian / Deep Charcoal (#0F172A) for a premium dark mode feel, alternating with Crisp White (#FFFFFF) for light sections.
- Accent Color: Metallic Gold / Brass (#D4AF37) for buttons, active links, and highlights.
- Secondary Text/Background: Slate Gray (#64748B) and Soft Silver (#F8FAFC).

3. TYPOGRAPHY
- Headings: 'Playfair Display' or 'Cinzel' (Sophisticated, elegant serif for a luxury feel).
- Body Text: 'Inter' or 'Montserrat' (Clean, highly legible sans-serif).

4. NAVBAR (HEADER)
- Style: Fixed (sticky) at the top, transparent on load, transitions to a glassmorphism blur effect (backdrop-filter: blur) upon scrolling.
- Left: Logo text "ZEAL" (bold) and "by Kasana" (lighter/smaller), or a sleek geometric building logo.
- Center Links: Home, About, Amenities, Location, Contact. Smooth scroll to sections.
- Right: "Enquire Now" CTA button (Gold background, Charcoal text, rounded corners, slight shadow).

5. ANIMATIONS & INTERACTIONS
- Page Load: Hero text fades in and slides up (Staggered entrance). Hero background image has a subtle, continuous slow-zoom effect.
- Scroll Effects (Intersection Observer): Sections fade in and slide up from the bottom as they enter the viewport (duration: 0.8s, easing: ease-out).
- Hover States: 
  - Nav links get a golden underline that expands from the center.
  - Buttons scale up by 1.05x with an enhanced drop shadow.
  - Amenity cards lift up slightly (-5px translate-Y) with a glowing border.

6. CONTENT STRUCTURE & SECTIONS

A. Hero Section (100vh)
- Background: High-resolution full-bleed image/render of a striking, modern glass-facade commercial skyscraper against a dusk sky.
- Overlay: Dark gradient overlay (from bottom and left) to ensure text readability.
- Headline: "Premium Grade A Commercial Spaces" (Large, bold, white).
- Sub-headline: "Elevate your business presence at TechZone 4." (Slightly smaller, light gray).
- CTA Buttons: Two side-by-side buttons -> "Explore Spaces" (Solid Gold) and "Download Brochure" (Outline White).

B. About / Overview Section
- Layout: 50/50 split (Text on left, Image on right).
- Image: A luxurious interior lobby with high ceilings and marble finishes.
- Content: "Where Vision Meets Architecture." Description emphasizing state-of-the-art facilities, sustainable design, and the perfect ecosystem for corporate growth.

C. Amenities & Features
- Layout: A 3-column CSS Grid.
- Styling: Light gray background with white cards for each amenity.
- Items (with modern SVG icons):
  1. 24/7 Premium Security & Surveillance
  2. High-Speed Intelligent Elevators
  3. Smart Automated Parking
  4. Green Building / Eco-friendly Design
  5. 100% Power Backup
  6. Premium Cafeteria & Lounge Areas

D. Location Section (TechZone 4)
- Layout: Centered heading followed by a stylized interactive map or a high-quality map graphic.
- Overlay Card: A floating glassmorphism card over the map detailing proximity to key landmarks (Metro Station: 5 mins, Expressway: 2 mins, International Airport: 45 mins).

E. Contact & Footer
- Background: Dark charcoal (#0F172A).
- Content (Left): Contact Form (Name, Email, Phone, Company, Message) with sleek floating labels and a Gold submit button.
- Content (Right): Contact info (Phone, Email, TechZone 4 Address).
- Footer Bottom: Copyright text "© 2026 Kasana Builders. All rights reserved.", social media links, and legal policies.
